## the Causes of Napoleon's failure
In 1812, French's emperor, Napoleon Eonaparte, led his Grand Army into Russia with adequate preparation. But he didnot make it to occupy Russia.    
Seemingly, what stopped him from devastating Russia is the raw, bitter, bleak Russian winter. The Russian even need not stand and fight, which would lead them to lose a lot of
Russian soldiers. They only retreated, burning their crops and homes, leaving the French facing the approching winter, and the winter defeated their opponents instead. If we go back to the source, we can find that it was Napoleon who didnnot take the weather into account. It was his error that caused his fiasco.       
As a military genius, if Napoleon thought more before he launched a war, or if he didnot be so arrogant, he may not have such a big loss.
